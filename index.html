Snoopy, our favorite beagle!

From faithful pet to World War II fighter pilot and everything in between.

Created by Charles M. Schultz in 1950 for his Peanutscomic strip.

Schultz fashioned Snoopy after his childhood dog, Spike.


Snoopy is recognized around the world as being somewhat mischevious,
gluttonous and lazy at times but overall he's a kindhearted, loyal friend.
